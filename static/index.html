GridWar

Real-time territory game with hidden mines, live spectators, and a global hall of fame.

Create Lobby

Choose a map and start a 4-player lobby.

How To Play
  1. Create the lobby and copy the share link for your friends.
  2. First 4 players become active players. Anyone after that joins as a spectator.
  3. All 4 players must press Ready Up before the match countdown starts.
  4. Click empty cells to claim territory once the game begins.
  5. Drag to pan and use the mouse wheel or trackpad to zoom around the board.
  6. Watch for hidden mines; hitting one wipes a 3x3 area and opens those cells again.
  7. Highest score at the end of the timer wins, with score based on owned territory.
